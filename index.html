go for it, prussia!
by alex zhu and amy li

rules:
- you will have to balance 2 main stats; iron, representing the strength of the economy, and blood, representing the strength of prussian arms and diplomatic power over others.
- you additionally must manage threat, which represents the concert of europe. if it becomes too high they will move against you.
- lastly, you must cultivate relationships with your fellow german states. make sure they like you!
- make the right choice given what happened historically to win!
